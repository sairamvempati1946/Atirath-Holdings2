Production Process

From Biomass to Clean Fuel

A 5-stage process transforms Napier Grass into high-purity Compressed Bio Gas.

1
Biomass Collection
Napier grass harvested from farms and transported
2
Pre-treatment
Shredding, sizing and moisture conditioning
3
Anaerobic Digestion
Microbes break down biomass releasing raw biogas
4
Biogas Purification
CO₂, H₂S removed — methane purity raised to 95%+
5
CBG Compression
Gas compressed to 200–250 bar for distribution
