Advisory Board

Eminent leaders providing strategic direction and industry expertise.

Sri Nara Chandra Babu Naidu Garu

Hon'ble Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh

Visionary leader who has championed the development of renewable energy infrastructure in Andhra Pradesh. Under his leadership, the state has emerged as a hub for clean energy investment, with Atirath's landmark 25-plant MoU signed in November 2025.

Dr. K. S. Bhatia

Former Secretary, Ministry of New & Renewable Energy

Former senior bureaucrat instrumental in shaping India's bioenergy policy framework. Provides strategic guidance on regulatory compliance, government relations, and SATAT scheme implementation. Played a key role in drafting the CBG Blending Obligation policy.

Prof. R. K. Pachauri

Distinguished Fellow, TERI

Renowned energy economist with expertise in sustainable development and climate policy. Advises on carbon finance, international partnerships, and technology roadmaps. Previously served on the UN's Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change (IPCC).

Sanjay Mehta

Former CEO, Indian Oil Corporation (Retd.)

Industry veteran with over 35 years of experience in oil & gas sector. Provides strategic counsel on CBG offtake, distribution networks, and partnership development with public-sector oil marketing companies under the SATAT framework.

Dr. V. P. Singh

Former Director, ICAR - Indian Institute of Sugarcane Research

Agricultural scientist specializing in energy crop cultivation. Advises on Napier Grass varietal development, pest management, and sustainable farming practices that maximize biomass yield while maintaining soil health.

Neelam Dhawan

Former Managing Director, HP India

Corporate leader with extensive experience in scaling operations and building institutional partnerships. Provides strategic guidance on organizational development, corporate governance, and investor relations.
