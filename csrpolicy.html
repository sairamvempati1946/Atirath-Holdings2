Policy Framework

Our CSR Philosophy

Our Vision

  • Create sustainable livelihoods in rural communities
  • Promote clean energy access for all sections of society
  • Build self-reliant villages through skill development
  • Protect and regenerate natural ecosystems

Legal Compliance

  • Companies Act, 2013 Section 135: Mandatory CSR compliance
  • CSR Committee: Board-level oversight and governance
  • Annual Reporting: Transparent impact assessment
  • Implementation: Direct and through implementation partners
Impact Areas

Our CSR Focus Areas

We channel our CSR efforts into five key areas that align with our core business values and community needs.

Education & Skill Development
Supporting rural education, vocational training, and digital literacy programs for youth and women.
Rural Livelihoods
Empowering farmers through sustainable agriculture, Napier grass cultivation, and market linkages.
Environmental Sustainability
Afforestation, water conservation, and promoting clean energy adoption in rural areas.
Healthcare & Sanitation
Mobile health clinics, awareness camps, and improving WASH facilities in villages.
Women Empowerment
Self-help groups, entrepreneurship training, and financial literacy programs.
Ongoing Initiatives

Our CSR Projects

Making a difference through targeted interventions in communities where we operate.

Napier Grass Farmer Livelihood Program
Telangana & Andhra Pradesh
Training and supporting 500+ farmers to cultivate Napier grass as a cash crop, providing guaranteed buyback and doubling farmer incomes.
Rural Digital Literacy Mission
Medak, Telangana
Setting up digital learning centers in 20 villages, providing computer education and skill training for 1,000 rural youth.
Jal Shakti - Water Conservation
Nalgonda District
Reviving traditional water bodies, constructing check dams, and promoting rainwater harvesting in water-scarce regions.
Women SHG & Entrepreneurship
Multiple Districts
Forming 50 self-help groups, providing microfinance linkages, and training women in small business management.
Mobile Health Clinics
Rural Telangana
Operating 2 mobile health vans providing free medical check-ups, medicines, and health awareness in 50+ villages.
Clean Energy Access
Remote Villages
Installing solar lighting in off-grid communities and promoting clean cooking solutions.

Our Journey

CSR Milestones

2025

CSR Foundation Year

Formal CSR policy adopted. Initial allocation of ₹2.5 crore for community programs. Launched farmer livelihood program.

2026

Expansion Phase

Expanded to 3 districts. 500+ farmers trained. Digital literacy centers established. Mobile health clinics launched.

2027

Target: 10,000+ Lives

Scale programs to 10 districts. Partner with 5 implementation NGOs. Achieve 10,000+ direct beneficiaries.

2030

Sustainable Impact Vision

Create self-sustaining community models. Achieve carbon neutrality. Empower 25,000+ rural households.
